This project is a settler-style resource management and village-building game developed in Unreal Engine using C++ during my 3rd semester.

The player assigns workers to houses and production buildings, generates resources such as wood and stone, and uses those resources to construct new structures and expand the settlement.

The core gameplay loop focuses on worker assignment, simple economy management and progression. The main goal is to continuously expand the village and eventually construct the City Hall, which acts as the final milestone while still allowing continued play afterwards.
