Castle Hunt is a small 2D platformer developed in Unity using C# during my 1st semester.

The player can run, jump, fight enemies and interact with the environment. One key mechanic allows cutting down vines, causing stones to fall and destroy parts of the level.

The goal is to complete the level by navigating obstacles, managing health and defeating enemies. This project mainly focused on learning the fundamentals of Unity, character movement and basic combat systems.
